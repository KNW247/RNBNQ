Recipes

Here are downloadable PDF versions of recipes featured in my Brew Day videos. These are not theoretical or overly polished — just real-world beers that I've brewed, refined, and entered into competition.

  • Love's Eternal Pale Ale — A balanced pale ale brewed for the weddings of some close friends. One of my earliest brew day recipes.
  • Vienna Lager — My most awarded lager to date, with medals in 8 of 11 comps including BOS2 and BOS3. Top score: 43.
  • Mexican Lager — Based on a pro recipe from Washington state. 3 golds, 3 bronzes, and a high score of 41 so far.
  • Munich Helles — A clean, drinkable Helles that's still evolving. Current best: gold and silver with a top score of 41.
  • American Wheat — Newer recipe with only a few comps under its belt, but already snagged a gold and top score of 39.
  • Rauchbier — My personal favorite. A smoked lager that bridges Spezial and Schlenkerla styles. So far: 1 gold, 1 bronze.
