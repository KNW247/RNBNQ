Brew Days

Each video below captures a full brew day, often featuring recipes that have gone on to earn medals in competition. These are real sessions from my garage brewery — the wins, the tweaks, and everything in between.

Love's Eternal Pale Ale

One of my early brew day videos, this Pale Ale was brewed for the weddings of close friends. A sentimental batch with solid process behind it. [Recipe PDF]

Vienna Lager

A competition workhorse. This recipe has medaled in 8 of 11 comps, including two Best of Show placements and a top score of 43. Clean, malty, and reliable. [Recipe PDF]

Mexican Lager

Based on a pro-designed recipe from Washington State, this one has earned 3 golds, 3 bronzes, and an honorable mention. Top score: 41. [Recipe PDF]

Munich Helles

Still dialing this one in, but it's already earned a gold and silver with a top score of 41. Light, crisp, and promising. [Recipe PDF]

American Wheat

A newer recipe that's already showing promise. Took gold in one of its first three comps with a top score of 39. [Recipe PDF]

Rauchbier

My favorite beer. Crafted to land between the intensity of Schlenkerla and the subtlety of Spezial. Two comps in and it already has a gold and bronze. Top score of 40 is promising as a new competitor in my stable. [Recipe PDF]
