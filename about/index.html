About the Brewer

Doug “Old Man” Walker brewed his first all-grain batch on July 31, 2021 using a Grainfather G40. What started as a curiosity quickly turned into a garage project, then into a garage brewery — complete with a dedicated sink, PEX plumbing, a chiller, a sparge tank, and over 300 pounds of grain in rotation.

He brews under the name Rusty Nut Brew ’n Que, a nod to the busted knuckles and late-night sessions that take place in the third bay of his garage.
