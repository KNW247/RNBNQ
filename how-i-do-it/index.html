How I Do It — Real Brewing. Real Gear. My Way.

This isn’t the way. It might not even be the best way.
It’s just my way — how I brew, clean, and build things with the gear I have and the space I’m in.

This series walks through how I approach different parts of the brewing process, solve problems, and make it work in the real world.

I’ll sometimes shout out gear or producers who support the homebrewing community and the clubs I’m part of. These aren’t paid promotions — just genuine nods to folks doing right by brewers.
