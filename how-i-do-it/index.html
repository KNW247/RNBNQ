How I Do It – Video Series

Episode 1: How I Clean My Fermenter (CIP Build)

This is the first episode of How I Do It — a series where I walk through my actual brewing gear and processes.

In this video, I walk through exactly how I clean my fermenter using my DIY CIP (Clean-in-Place) setup. The heart of this system is a ¾ HP, 3300 GPH pump—delivering 4–6× the flow rate of most homebrew CIP kits. It’s industrial-level cleaning power, built for a homebrew budget.

This isn’t a parts list or theory—this is how I actually clean my gear. No heavy lifting. Just fast, effective cleaning that gets me back to brewing.
